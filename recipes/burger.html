Burger

Description:Juicy Burger.

Ingredients:

  • 1lb ground beef
  • 4 buns
  • 4 onion slices
  • 4 tomato slices
  • 12 pickle slices
  • 4 slices of cheese
  • 4 tablespoons of ketchup

Instructions

  1. Heat pan and add oil.
  2. Roll ground beef into 4 balls and roll into patties.
  3. Put patties on pan/
  4. Cook to desired temperature.
  5. When done, add cheese and cook for additonal 30s until it melts.
  6. Place lettuce and tomato on bun.
  7. Then, add the cheese patty on top of lettuce and tomato.
  8. Add onions, pickles, and ketchup on top of patty.
  9. Finish with top bun and repeat to make 4 burgers.
