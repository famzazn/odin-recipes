Kebab

Description: Fresh off the grill kebab.

Ingredients:

  • 6 wooden skewers
  • 1lb chicken sliced into 1 inch cubes
  • 1 bell pepper, sliced into 1 inch squares
  • 1 lemon, sliced into 1 inch cubes
  • 1/2 onion, slices into 1 inch squares
  • store bought tzaziki sauce

Instructions

  1. Heat charcoal grill.
  2. Place ingredients on skewers in this order: chicken, pepper, lemon, onion, repeat.
  3. Place six skewers in grill.
  4. Cook until chicken is golden brown.
