Kebab

Description: Fresh off the grill kebab..

Ingredients:

  • Store-bought pizza dough
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 cup pizza sauce
  • 8oz shredded mozzarella cheese
  • [Optional] 2.5oz pepperoni slices
  • 2 tablespoons shredded parmesan cheese

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 550 degrees.
  2. Roll out dough into 16 in diameter circle.
  3. Put parchment paper on tray and spread olive oil on parchment paper.
  4. Transfer dough onto parchment paper.
  5. Spread pizza sauce evenly all over dough.
  6. Evenly distribute shredded cheese, both mozzarella and parmesan cheese.
  7. [Optional]Add pepperoni if desired.
  8. Transfer pizza to oven and bake for 8-12 minutes.
